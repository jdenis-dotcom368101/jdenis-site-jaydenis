Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about AI support

What is an AI diagnostic for a business? +

An AI diagnostic evaluates your organisation's digital maturity, identifies priority AI use cases by department (HR, sales, production, finance) and defines a roadmap with ROI prioritisation and a change management plan. JDENIS Consulting offers tailored AI diagnostics for SMEs and large corporations in Toulouse and across France.

What AI training programmes do you offer for businesses? +

We offer intensive 1 to 5-day programmes on AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Mistral), advanced prompting, business process automation and data governance. Training is aimed at executives, managers and operational teams. On-site in Toulouse or remote, anywhere in France and internationally.

What is data governance and the EU AI Act? +

Data governance refers to the policies and processes that govern how a company uses data. The AI Act is the European Union regulation on artificial intelligence. JDENIS Consulting helps you achieve compliance: AI usage policies, risk classification, data audits and legal & ethical frameworks.

Do you only work in Toulouse? +

Based in Toulouse, Jérôme Denis works across France and internationally. AI training and diagnostics can be delivered on-site or remotely. He also lectures at 7 higher education institutions including TBS Education, YNOV, Purpan, MediaSchool, LISAA, Digital Campus and Institut Limayrac.

How does AI training with JDENIS Consulting work? +

Every training starts with a discovery call to understand your specific needs. The programme is then customised: hands-on workshops with ChatGPT, Claude or Mistral, real-world use cases tailored to your industry, prompt engineering exercises, and a post-training action plan. Sessions can be individual or group-based, from 1 to 5 days.
